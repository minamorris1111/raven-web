Permanent action

Delete your account

You can delete your Raven account at any time. Once deleted, all your data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

How to delete
From the app (recommended)

Open Raven → Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete Account → confirm. Your account is deleted immediately.

By email

Send a request to maiphamtn@hotmail.com with the subject "Delete Account" and your registered email address. We'll process it within 7 days.

What gets deleted: your profile, progress, XP, word bank, friends list, and all associated data. Subscription cancellation is separate — please cancel through the App Store or Google Play before requesting deletion.
